# Site brief

## What this site is
A marketing website for a Bay Area HVAC-focused offering: “AI Discovery” / entity clarity + structured service pages + listings consistency + schema + tracking.

## Primary goal
Get qualified HVAC owners/operators to book a demo/snapshot via Calendly.

## Primary CTA
Get a free “AI Readiness Snapshot” (10-min Loom) — limited weekly spots.

## Conversion path
Homepage → Pricing / Demo case study → Calendly booking.

## Constraints
- 5–10 pages
- No contact form (Calendly + email is sufficient)
- Copy is not final; expect small tweaks.
- Design system is TBD; should feel modern, credible, “clean B2B local services,” and fast.

## Audience (working)
Bay Area HVAC teams seeking more booked estimates and fewer unqualified calls.

## Proof status
Early-stage: onboarding first partners; demo case study must be clearly labeled as demo.
